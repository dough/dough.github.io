My mother was a registered nurse and able to help on occasion with understanding what I was doing. Paul actually ended up badly, sadly, losing an eye and laTer his life. I had looked forward to chemistry in high school. However the very experiences and probably very good teacher resigned the year before I took the course, and the new teacher, although very caring, did not draw my attention to the field as a future way of life. She was also rather naive. When sh passed around a container of Mercury so we all could sense it's properties, and I stole(sadly) some to take hom for my lab by pouring it in my shirt pocket, upong bending over outside in the hall I learned why it is called "quicksilver"!.
