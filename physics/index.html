This gives the 8 vertices RUF,RUN,RDF,RDN,LUF,LUN,LDF,LDN and 6 faces R,U,F,L,D,N familiar to us all (since the time of Plato) and 12 edges (labelled in appropriate ways by a pair of faces. Thinking topologically of the box with inside and outside, it is easy to iew each edge as a small tube between vertices, and this picture shows some of those tubes. Now seeing "event-particles' as tiny volumes entering or leaving the box at LDN/RUN, travelling one wa or the other along that tube(colored magenta),and coloring the entry/exit edges apropriately red or blue indicating a gain or loss of something(say energy?) and using green for intermediate paths, we find the pattern (that much of my topological career has been buit upon). This pattern can be viewed in terms of singlet-triplet rather easily,and that is something spectroscopists have seen, and quantum-theorist have tried to describe by viewing only formulas. Lets say you are changing energy going between LDN and RUF. You follow the pattern R--B, and to cross that gap you have only green. But following green you can go straight or visit some neighbors, but only by singlet-or-triplet. More detailed explanations will not change what actually happened.
